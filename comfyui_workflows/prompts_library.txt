## ======================
## 4个视角方向的修改
## ======================

### DIRECTION: W (向上/背面)
Add to prompt: "back view, walking away from camera, back of skull visible, cape flowing, shield visible on left side from behind, sword on right side"

### DIRECTION: S (向下/正面)  
Add to prompt: "front view, walking toward camera, skull front face visible, black eye sockets prominent, shield in left hand visible, sword in right hand"

### DIRECTION: A (向左)
Add to prompt: "left side view, facing left, left profile of skull, shield on left blocking body, sword behind on right side"

### DIRECTION: D (向右)
Add to prompt: "right side view, facing right, right profile of skull, sword visible in front on right, shield behind on left side"

## ======================
## ControlNet专用描述
## ======================

当使用OpenPose ControlNet时，在基础prompt后添加：
"matching the exact pose from control image, following bone structure precisely, limb positions accurate to reference"

当使用Depth ControlNet时：
"matching depth layers of reference, foreground shield, mid-ground body, background cape layers"

当使用Canny边缘时：
"following edge lines exactly, sharp contours matching reference, clean outlines"

## ======================
## 快速替换模板
## ======================

复制这个基础模板，只改 [POSE] 部分：

BASE_TEMPLATE = """
pixel art, chibi skeleton warrior, [POSE],
side view facing left, white skull left profile,
left arm holding round golden shield, right arm holding sword,
brown leather armor, tattered cape, skeletal hands,
64x64 pixel style, clean pixel linework, 
limited 16-bit color palette, sharp edges,
transparent background, single character,
retro 16-bit RPG game sprite, top-down view style
"""

NEGATIVE = """
realistic, photo, 3d render, blurry, smooth, gradient,
anti-aliasing, multiple characters, background,
text, watermark, modern art, high resolution
"""

[POSE] 替换为：
- "standing idle pose"
- "walking left leg forward mid-stride"
- "attacking slash motion"
- "shield blocking defensive"
- etc...
